How to Play & Controls

  • Objective: Clear all 5 static campaign Raid Sites marked on the map to complete your Saga!
  • World Map Navigation: Move using Arrow Keys / WASD or click adjacent tiles. Sail on sea tiles (fast, safe) or walk on land (slow, consumes more food).
  • Starvation Warning: If food reaches 0, every step taken on the World Map kills a random member of your band! Buy food at Towns.
  • Raid Sites (Local Maps): Explore the Fog of War to defeat monsters, raid burial mounds, and gather Magic Objects (Relics).
  • Lane Combat: Deploy your warriors. Warriors reaching the right side return to your band and yield +1 Gold. Warrior deaths are permanent!
  • Appease the Gods: Sacrifice Relics at Towns to raise God Favor tracks. Reaching 5/5 unlocks ultimate buffs, but angering them triggers dangerous curses.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • S / L: Save / Load game (on the World Map).
    • W: Blow the War Horn to pull enemies into combat from a distance (on Local Maps).
    • B: Toggle Viking Band Cargo Panel.
    • Q: Toggle God Favor / Quests screen.
    • 1 - 9: Select modal options.
    • Escape: Cancel, close panels, or leave locations.
