How to Play

  • World Map: Sail your drakkar across sea tiles (safe and fast) or walk on land (slow, consumes more food, triggers monster attacks).
  • Starvation: If food hits 0, each step on the World Map kills one random band member!
  • Raid Sites (10x10): Pre-generated layout with Fog of War. Defeat monsters, open chests, check burial mounds, or visit dolmen shrines to obtain Magic Objects.
  • Lane Combat (10x8): Automated lane battle. Deploy soldiers into lanes. Soldiers reaching the end give +1 Gold and return to your pool. Units are lost permanently on death.
  • The 5 Gods: Trade Magic Objects to advance god favor tracks. Reaching 5/5 unlocks permanent favor and ultimate buffs.
